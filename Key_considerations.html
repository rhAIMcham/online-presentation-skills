Consideration What to look for Why it matters
Audience familiarity Choose a platform your participants already know. Reduces confusion and technical delays during setup.
Interactivity needs Look for features like polls, breakout rooms and reactions. Keeps participants engaged and encourages collaboration.
Security and access Check if login is required and if waiting rooms or passcodes are available. Ensures data safety and a professional environment.
Ease of use Preference platforms with intuitive interfaces and minimal setup steps. Allows you to focus on content, not troubleshooting.
Technical requirements Confirm compatibility with devices, bandwidth and recording options. Prevents lags, poor video/audio quality or feature glitches.
Integration with other tools Check if the platform connects with your calendar, email or file-sharing tools. Simplifies scheduling and content sharing.
